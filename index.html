About me

I'm Laurie Ivan Bonete a full stack web developer and a polygloth in software programming since circa 2012. Currently based on Manila, Philippines, PH.

A proud alumnus of University of Santo Tomas in Information Technology. Had polished the existing skills I have in software development and it's cycle, and definitely still gathering more knowledge as I go with a couple of employeers through the years.

As I firmly believe that employment is not the end of all this, me and my colleagues build a startup software company named Codegum. We already had a handful of projects and there are still more coming in.

The sun is rising up in the horizon and illuminating the pasture.

Services

Application Development

Develops responsive web applications, desktop applications, or even mobile applications tailored for your business needs. Providing your more competitive edge than your business competitors.

Business Upgrade

Let's elimate or at least reduce processes on your business that is so labor intensive, time consuming, error prone, and resource expensive. We can do that by building softwares either small or big tools that will automate your business proesses.

Consulting
